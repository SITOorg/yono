How to use this navigator:
  • Hover over the center piece to find hot spots for expanding vertically and horizontally. Not all pieces have expansions.
  • Click a hotspot (expansion arrows) to expand the reveal the image inside (in that direction).
  • Click on any little peripheral squares or rectangles to collapse the structure to the image clicked.
  • Use keyboard keys! Use the W, A, S, and D keys as if they were arrows (like in PC games). W/S are vertical expansion/collapse. A/D are horizontal expansion/collapse. The [SPACEBAR] will start a random crawl through the Yono pieces. The arrow keys work too, but also scroll your browser.
