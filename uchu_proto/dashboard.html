List of Yonodes

This list shows slot availability for each yonode. They are listed in order from newest to oldest. The codes that look like "200_FOO" indicate the unique identifier for a yonode. Listed for each yonode are the other yonodes that bridge its halves horizontally and vertically. If a bridging slot hasn't been filled yet, it will be marked with the word "OPEN".
